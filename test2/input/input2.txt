All: We'll hear no more; to the Capitol!
First Citizen: To the Capitol! follow me.
Second Citizen: Peace, I say; be not rash.
Menenius: Good men, the wounds of your country are not balm'd by rage.
All: Speak, speak!
First Citizen: Show us the man; we'll answer him with our pikes.
Menenius: Your valour is a song sung out of season.
All: To the Capitol! to the Capitol!
